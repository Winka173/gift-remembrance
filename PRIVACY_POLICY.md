# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-04-18**

Gift Remembrance ("the app") is designed to be a private, on-device gift tracker. This policy explains exactly what data the app stores, what leaves your device, and what rights you have.

## What data is stored locally

All of the following is stored on your device only, in an encrypted MMKV key-value store and the app's sandboxed file system:

- People you add: names, relationships, optional notes, optional avatar photo.
- Occasions: birthdays, anniversaries, and other dates you track.
- Gifts: names, notes, prices, photos, dates, and the person each gift is associated with.
- App settings and preferences.

## What leaves your device

The app does not operate any server. There is no developer-controlled backend, no user account, no Google Sign-In, and no Google Drive OAuth integration.

Data can leave your device only in these two cases:

- **User-initiated backup.** If you choose iCloud backup (iOS) or Android SAF (Storage Access Framework) backup, the backup zip is written to a location you pick in your own cloud/file-system account. SAF is a local file-system API — it does not transmit data to us. We never receive a copy.
- **AdMob advertising identifiers.** The app shows ads via Google AdMob. If you consent to personalized ads (via the Google UMP consent form on Android or the ATT prompt on iOS), AdMob may access your advertising identifier. If you decline, the app requests non-personalized ads.

## Permissions the app asks for

- **Contacts.** Read only when you tap Import Contacts. Contacts are never uploaded anywhere.
- **Camera / Photo Library.** Used only when you pick a photo for a gift or person. Photos are stored only on your device.
- **Notifications.** All reminders are scheduled locally by the operating system. Notification content is never sent to any server.

## What the app does NOT do

- No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
- No crash reporting SDK.
- No user tracking beyond AdMob's standard advertising identifier.
- No account creation, no login, no sync.

## Your rights

- **Export all data.** Settings → Export Data produces a zip of your data you can share or save anywhere.
- **Delete all data.** Settings → Delete All Data wipes the MMKV store, all photos, and cancels scheduled notifications. This action is irreversible.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
